Rules

Your Tamagotchi will start as an Egg. After a short amount of time, your newborn will be there, asking you to take care of him.

Your goal is to keep him alive for as long as possible. To do so:

  • Make sure he is rested. Otherwise, he might fall asleep all of a sudden. Or die.
  • Feed him when he is hungry... But not too much, or you will have tons of poop to clean.
  • Keep him entertained. You do not want him to get bored to death.

If you feel up to the task, you may even create several Tamagotchis at the same time.
